About

Chimp Chomp is a design and research lab based in Surabaya, Indonesia. Powered by a couple of hyper monkeys honed in multidisciplinary areas from design, computer science, photography, social science and cultural studies, our clients and collaborators range from independent initiatives to multi-national corporations and organisations.
